LccTools

For app support information and our privacy policy, please see the support page. There are also pages on getting started for the Mac and iPhone/iPad versions.

LccTools helps you operated your LCC-connected model railroad. It provides:

  • A throttle for LCC-connected command stations such as the TCS CS-105.
  • A consist tool for making, modifying and breaking consists in the command station.
  • A turnout control that lets you throw turnouts and see their status on your layout.
  • A fast-clock display that shows the current (fast) time on your layout.
  • A configuration tool that lets to see and modify the configuration of your LCC nodes.
  • A monitor page to see traffic on your LCC network.
There are versions customized for iPhone, iPad and Mac. Run your trains from your iPhone, and take your iPad out to the layout to configure your nodes!

You can download LccTools for free from the App Store.
